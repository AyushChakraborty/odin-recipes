Lets make a tasty and healthy Lasagna!

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Lasagna is made by layering lasagna noodles with a savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese mixture, and shredded mozzarella, then baking until bubbly and golden. The dish combines the flavors of rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and seasoned meat for a comforting, hearty meal.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices, seasonings
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
